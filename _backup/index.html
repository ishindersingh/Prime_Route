Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide moving supplies?

We provide moving blankets and straps to secure your items during transit. If you need boxes or packing materials, please let us know in advance.

Is there a minimum hourly charge?

Yes, we have a 2-hour minimum charge for our hourly moving services to cover travel and dispatch costs.

Can I ride in the van?

For liability reasons, passengers are not permitted in the moving van. You will need to arrange your own transportation to the destination.
